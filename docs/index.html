Underlying Legs / WTI · RBOB · Heating Oil
Outright futures prices driving the spread calculations.
Methodology — 3:2:1 crack = [(2 × RBOB + 1 × HO) × 42 − 3 × WTI] / 3, expressed in $/bbl (RBOB & HO converted from $/gal at 42 gal/bbl). Gasoline crack = RBOB(bbl) − WTI. Distillate crack = HO(bbl) − WTI. VaR is 1‑day historical simulation on the trailing 252 daily spread changes. Z‑score and percentile rank are computed against the trailing 1‑year distribution.
Data — NYMEX continuous front‑month futures (CL=F, RB=F, HO=F), daily settlement, sourced via Yahoo Finance. For live risk decisions, confirm against your execution venue's settlement prices — continuous contracts can show roll‑related noise around expiry.
